Average Household Income
A very commonly asked question is "what is the average household income in the 22213 zip code?". The official term for the average household income is the mean household income. The mean household income is the total income of all households divided by the number of households.

The average (or mean) household income in 22213 is $207,722.00.

41.59% of households in the 22213 zip code are classed as high income households (making $200,000+ per year).



THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A MEDIAN AND AN AVERAGE INCOME
The difference between a median and mean household income is that the median income is the middle point of the set of numbers whereas the mean income is an average of all numbers.

Both metrics are important when looking at the income statistics for 22213 but because the median value doesn't depend on other numbers in the dataset, it doesn't change as much when some of the values are extremely low or extremely high.

Lower levels of unemployment is generally seen as a positive sign for the economy. It also makes the government in 22213 look good because it shows that they are doing a good job of creating jobs, working to capacity, being efficient, and keeping the economy strong.

Median Age in 22213
The median age is an interesting metric because it gives us an idea of the age distribution of people in the 22213 zip code. You know when you see a median age that it is the age where half of the population is older and half is younger than it.

For demographic studies, this can be used to infer and compare date to things such as birth rates, the age of parents, and so on.

An increasing median age can be a sign of an aging population, which can be a sign of a declining population. This is because older people tend to have fewer children and die more often than younger people.
