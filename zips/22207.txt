Average Household Income
A very commonly asked question is "what is the average household income in the 22207 zip code?". The official term for the average household income is the mean household income. The mean household income is the total income of all households divided by the number of households.

The average (or mean) household income in 22207 is $285,339.00.

52.69% of households in the 22207 zip code are classed as high income households (making $200,000+ per year).

THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A MEDIAN AND AN AVERAGE INCOME
The difference between a median and mean household income is that the median income is the middle point of the set of numbers whereas the mean income is an average of all numbers.

Both metrics are important when looking at the income statistics for 22207 but because the median value doesn't depend on other numbers in the dataset, it doesn't change as much when some of the values are extremely low or extremely high.

Per Capita Income
The per capita income is the average income of all people in the 22207 zip code. This is calculated by dividing the total income of all people in 22207 by the total number of people in 22207.

It differs from the average or mean household income because it includes all people in 22207 and not just the people who earn an income (i.e. children, retirees, unemployed, etc.).

Median Age in 22207
The median age is an interesting metric because it gives us an idea of the age distribution of people in the 22207 zip code. You know when you see a median age that it is the age where half of the population is older and half is younger than it.

For demographic studies, this can be used to infer and compare date to things such as birth rates, the age of parents, and so on.

An increasing median age can be a sign of an aging population, which can be a sign of a declining population. This is because older people tend to have fewer children and die more often than younger people.
